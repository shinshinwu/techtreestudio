The Problem

"We don't have a website to help spread the word about our initiative."

PublicBnB is modernizing the public housing system with the creation of their new on-line platform, making it simpler than ever to participate as a host or tenant in affordable housing. However, they were not able to concurrently design and develop a website while they were still working on their main product.

The solution

techtree studio built publicBnB a website that captured and conveyed their own essense: warm, professional, and accesible.

TECHTREE Studio involved PublicBnB in the design process from the very beginnign to integrate PublicBnB's industry insight into the project and we came away with the best of both worlds--a clean, modern, and fresh interface that is coupled with an inviting and warm feel.

The Result

"We are as proud of our website as we are of our own application."

PublicBnB Founder
