Our Process

Client Discovery

Fill out our Website Planner and we will schedule a meeting or a call to go over your answers to learn more about your business and your online marketing goals.

Select a Plan

We will then develop a proposal and you will have the opportunity to choose from our recommended web design service plan and Weebly's website theme's.

Design & Preview

We will then build out a live preview based upon the Weebly template you have chosen. We will customize the template based upon your requirements.

Project Management

Utilizing a proven project management plan, we will complete your website based upon the website plan and other TechTreeStudio services you have selected.

Setup & Quality Testing

Before your website goes live, we will then make sure the usability of your website is optimal for your audience and we will make sure to implement the standard Weebly SEO features.

Ongoing Optimization

We will then provide you with training on Weebly's Content Management System, or you can sign up for a managed plan where we take care of any ongoing support you many need.
