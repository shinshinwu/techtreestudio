Our Six-step Proprietary Production Process

  • 1. Discover

    We conduct research in your domain to better understand the problem at hand and we work with you to establish requirements, uncover opportunities, and identify possible solutions.

  • 2. Design

    We synthesize our discovery process and design a solution that is tailored to fit your budget, desired turn-around, and envisioned use case. We then iterate on this design to refine it, ensure that you love it, and prime it for development.

  • 3. Develop

    Code gets writen, systems get built, and designs come to life. We take careful measure to ensure best practices are administered, tried and proven technologies are used, and in the process a market-ready product is forged.

  • 4. Deploy

    Deployment is always a two-step process. First, as a quality assurance measure, we administer a private deployment to reveal and resolve any problems that may surface during real-world use. We then proceed to deploy your system publicly once we ensure it works as intended.

  • 5. Discern

    We monitor your system to ensure it runs well, it scales as needed, and it fulfills the purpose it was built for. We also qualify any opportunities for additional features, optimizations, or enhancements.

  • 6. Determine

    We conclude the production process, determine any necessary follow-up steps, and transition into maintaining your system, if that is what is desired. The process of improvement is never ending and we are always ready to discover how we can help you branch out into your next level.
