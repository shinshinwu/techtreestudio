The Problem

"We want to give our company a new image, modernize our current website, and have it be mobile-friendly."

PPS was an established company with a severaly outdated online precense. Their business had declined year over year, their website was not mobile friendly, and their website's look was comprimising their ability to generate new business. To make matters worse, they had a very limited budget.

The Solution

techtree studio helped Perfect Personal Statement build a better on-line experience from the ground up.

PPS initially wanted to hire us to simply make their website mobile friendly, because they thought that was all they could afford. Instead, we set them up with our small business starter plan and built a new and improved online experience for them from the ground up.

The finished product matched their budget and surpassed their expectations.
