The Problem

"We have an idea but are not technologically inclined. Can you help us build a prototype and bring our idea to life?"

The team at PublicAd Space had a brilliant idea for a startup, they wanted to create a one-stop market place for the Outdoor advertisement industry; however, they did not know what an MVP was (minimum viable product), nor did they have the technological proficiency to build one as their first product.

The Solution

We helped bring PublicAd Space to life by engineering a product that would help them test their assumptions, validate their idea, and enter the market.

In order to allow PublicAd Space to reach as many users as possible, we decided to build their platform as a responsive web application that works and looks great on all devices--mobile, tablets, laptops, desktops. In addition, we leveraged the Google Maps API to allow their users to see all public ads on a map and utilized geosearching to find inventory based on your desired proximity to the location of your choice

As a cherry on top, we even gave their users the ability to snap an image of any outdoor media sign, pick up their exact location with geolocation, and set and save the street view to capture the exact orientation of the advertisement.

The Result
